Belief Transformer

Replacing harmful beliefs with healthy ones can be tricky, but this tool helps you make changes.

Have you ever wished you could just brainwash yourelf? As we mature, we gain new understanding of the world, and we adopt new mindsets and values. We re-evaluate our behaviors and figure how useful each is to us - and decide to adopt it. It goes well for a bit... But then we flip-flop. In response to certain triggers and conditions we can slip back into harmful old patterns of thinking and acting. We may realize that choosing a new belief would improve our lives, but on a gut level, the old belief still feels much more real.

What's the process?

Here are the steps to changing a belief:.

  1. Start by identifying the subconscious belief that controls your reactions or sabotages your behavior.
  2. Then, identify the alternative (opposite) belief that is healthier / more beneficial to you
  3. Identify the roots of each of these beliefs - in other words, the proof and references
  4. Discredit the sources of proof for the unhealthy belief
  5. Create a reflex responses to nagging thoughts
  6. Come up with new sources of proof
  7. Find new references
